FAQs

Questions, answered.

How fast will I get my quote?
We review every request and reply within one working day with a fixed-price quote, lead time, and the shop making your part. No instant-quote bots.
Is there a minimum order quantity?
No minimum for prototypes, we'll happily make a single part. We also run full production from tens to 10,000+ units, so you can start small and scale with the same team.
Is my design and IP protected?
Yes. Everything moves under a UK-law NDA before any work begins. Your drawings and files are shared only with the vetted factory making your part, and nothing else.
What processes and materials do you offer?
CNC milling & turning, sheet metal & fabrication, 3D printing, finishing and packaging, plus in-house design. Aluminium, steel, stainless, titanium, brass and engineering plastics, with tolerances to ±0.01 mm.
I don't have CAD files, can you still help?
Absolutely. Our in-house design team can reverse-engineer an existing part, 3D-scan complex geometry, or build a production-ready model from a sketch, photo or brief.
How much can I save versus UK manufacturing?
Typically 40–60% on the same part, to the same drawing and tolerance, through our vetted global partner network, with a British engineer holding the quality and the contract.
What if a part fails inspection?
Every batch is inspected against your drawing before it ships, so issues are caught before they reach you. If something isn't right, we work with you to put it right. Your contract is with Forgeline Ltd in the UK.
Do you handle shipping, duty and VAT?
Yes, DDP delivery to your door or warehouse with duty and VAT handled, or DDU if you'd rather handle import yourself. One fixed-price invoice in GBP or EUR.
